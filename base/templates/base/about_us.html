About CampusConnect

Centralizing Your University Event Experience.

CampusConnect is a centralized event management portal developed for MM(DU) University.

Our goal is to simplify the way students and faculty connect with events happening across the campus â€” whether academic, cultural, or technical.

We built this platform after noticing that students often miss out on opportunities due to scattered communication on notice boards or WhatsApp groups. Faculty members also face challenges in handling registrations and keeping track of participants. CampusConnect provides a simple, reliable, and professional solution to these problems.

With features like event browsing, online registration with instant email confirmations, and a newsletter system for upcoming events, students can stay informed and engaged. Faculty members can easily manage events through the admin dashboard, ensuring smoother organization.

Our vision is to create a connected and transparent event culture at MM(DU), where students never miss opportunities to learn, participate, and grow.
